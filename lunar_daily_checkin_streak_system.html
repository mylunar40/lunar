Phase 1: Daily Check-In Design

The Core Problem

Women won't return to an app that doesn't ask "How are you today?" Daily check-ins create emotional intimacy with the app, not just habit loops.

Recommended Structure: Intent-Aware Multi-Dimension Check-In

Step 1: Emotional Touchpoint (2-3 seconds)

┌─────────────────────────────────┐ │ Morning 🌙 │ │ │ │ How are you feeling today? │ │ │ │ [😢] [😐] [😊] [🤗] │ │ │ │ Struggling Okay Good Better │ └─────────────────────────────────┘

Design Philosophy

  • Emoji, not words: Faster interaction, more accessible
  • 4 states, not 10: Research shows 4-7 options optimal
  • Horizontal scroll: Feels lightweight, not "form"
  • No "perfect" state: Avoid toxic positivity

Psychology

  • Taps into loss aversion (don't want to miss check-in)
  • Minimal friction: 1 tap = complete
  • Emotional validation: Each state normalized

Step 2: Secondary Dimension (3-5 seconds, conditional)

If [😢] "Struggling" selected:
"What's weighing on you today?"
[Physical] [Mental] [Both] [Not sure]
If [😊] "Good" selected:
"What made today good?"
[Something I did] [Someone helped me] [Small joy] [Can't pinpoint it]
If [🤗] "Better" selected:
"You're smiling—was it:"
[Chat with Lunar] [Journaling] [Community] [Something else]

Why Secondary Dimension?

  • Rich data for insights (without added friction)
  • Validates user's specific experience
  • Informs AI conversation routing
  • Captures "progress moments"

Phase 2: Streak System Design

Problem: Avoid Toxic Gamification

❌ DON'T DO:
• Leaderboards ("You're #42 in Breakup Recovery")
• Competitive streaks (healing isn't competition)
• Severe punishment (one miss = reset to zero)
• Shame-based notifications
✅ DO:
• Personal progress ("Your Healing Continues")
• Psychological milestones (non-arbitrary)
• Resilience recognition (streaks CAN break)
• Celebration of consistency

Recommended Streak Architecture

Visual Prominence: Home Screen Top-Left

┌──────────────────────────┐ │ 🔥 7-Day Healing Streak │ │ ──────────────────── │ │ ████░░░░░░ │ │ Your momentum matters. │ │ │ │ Next: Day 8 = Committed │ │ Day 14 = Resilient │ └──────────────────────────┘

Three Streak Types (Intent-Specific)

Type 1: Daily Consistency Streak

Intent Name Purpose
Breakup "Healing Streak" Shows commitment to recovery
Anxiety "Grounded Streak" Shows resilience against anxiety
Pregnancy "Connection Streak" Shows commitment to baby journey
Cycle "Wisdom Streak" Shows cycle awareness
Self Love "Self-Care Streak" Shows self-honoring

Mechanics

  • Increases by 1 each day user completes check-in
  • Resets if 2+ days missed (not "broken" after 1 miss)
  • Visual indicator if at risk (gray out on day 2 missed)

Psychological Foundation

  • 2-day forgiveness acknowledges life happens
  • "Reset" language more empowering than "lost"
  • Builds resilience, not perfectionism

Type 2: Weekly Milestone Streaks

Level Breakup Anxiety Pregnancy Self Love
Week 1 Starting Strong Finding Calm Embracing Changes Beginning Again
Week 2 Deepening Grounded Growing Connection Recognizing Worth
Week 4 Halfway There Resilient Mid-Journey Owning It
Week 12 Three Months Quarter Year Three Months Transformed

Type 3: Healing Milestone Streak (Psychological)

Not a "streak" in traditional sense—milestones that celebrate emotional progress:

🎯 Day 7: "First Week of Healing"
"You showed up for yourself even when it hurt."

🎯 Day 21: "Three Weeks of Progress"
"Your resilience is becoming visible."

🎯 Day 60: "Two Months of Growth"
"You're not 'over it' yet—and that's okay. You're moving."

🎯 Day 90: "Ready for the Next Chapter"
"Breakups reshape us. Here's what you've built."

Phase 3: Progress System Design

Psychological Framework

Users need to feel agency ("I'm making progress") not just metrics ("I did 7 check-ins").

Four Types of Progress

1. Consistency Progress (Quantitative)

"You've checked in 23 days in a row.
That's 23 days you showed up for yourself."

Not: "You're 65% toward 30 days!"

2. Emotional Progress (Qualitative)

"Your feelings this week:
Monday: 😢 → Wednesday: 😐 → Friday: 😊

You're moving. That's healing."

3. Behavioral Progress (Actions Taken)

"This week, you:
• Journaled 5 times (courage)
• Talked to Lunar 12 times (self-compassion)
• Joined 2 community posts (connection)

These are all forms of healing."

4. Milestone Progress (Transformational)

Day Breakup Recovery Anxiety Self Love
7 First Week 5 Grounded Days Ten Days of Kindness
21 Three Weeks Two Weeks of Calm I See My Worth
60 I'm Smiling Again Anxiety Toolkit Works I Choose Myself First
90 Ready for Next Chapter — —

Progress Moments: Unexpected Celebrations

Keep going.

Phase 4: Personalization By Intent

Check-In Customization Matrix

Breakup Recovery User

Interaction Question Focus
Primary "How are you feeling?" Emotional focus
Secondary (if struggling) "Is it the breakup?" Specific validation
Secondary (if good) "What helped you feel stronger?" Coping identification
Example Daily Message:

"Good morning. How's your heart today?

Remember: Some days you'll ache.
Some days you'll smile.
Both are part of healing.

Let's figure out today together."

Anxiety & Stress User

Interaction Question Focus
Primary "How's your mind?" Anxiety-specific
Secondary (if struggling) "Racing thoughts or physical tension?" Anxiety subtype
Secondary (if good) "What grounding helped?" Technique identification
Example Daily Message:

"Good morning. Let's check in with your nervous system.

You have tools. You have resilience.
Some days the anxiety is louder.
Some days your strength is louder.

What does today need?"

Pregnancy User

Interaction Question Focus
Primary "How's your body + mind?" Both matter
Secondary (if struggling) "Physical or emotional or both?" Specificity
Secondary (if good) "Any baby moments today?" Celebrate connection
Example Daily Message:

"Good morning, mama.

You're carrying life—your body and heart
are doing extraordinary things.

How are you feeling in this moment?"

Cycle Wellness User

Interaction Question Focus
Primary "How's your cycle energy?" Cycle-aware language
Secondary (if struggling) "What phase are you in?" Phase context
Secondary (if good) "Is this peak energy day?" Celebrate ovulation/follicular
Example Daily Message:

"Good morning.

Your cycle is your superpower.
Today, what phase energy do you have?

Let's work WITH your body, not against it."

  • Weekly Milestone Streaks (3 days)

IGNORE (For This MVP)
